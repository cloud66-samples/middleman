Episode 3 of LRUG Nights will take place on Tuesday, 21st August at the Fitzroy Tavern on Charlotte Street. Come along for the usual mix of chat, boozing and ribaldry (and only the slightest mentions of metaprogramming). If you can't make it, this is the kind of thing you might be in for.
